Updates

May 27, 2020: Welcome to the ~new and improved website~ This website was originally created in November 2019 by Sophia Hoenig ('22) and Hannah Finn-Erb ('20). With school finished for the year, we have finally finished building it. We hope to use this over the next year (and years to come) to provide updates about team information that cannot necessarily be shared over social media.

Sadly our season came to an abrupt end due to the global pandemic. However, prior to the end of the season, we had an undefeated record which we planned to maintain all the way to nationals!

This spring we also put forward our first ever nominee for the Donovan Award: Sarah "Bloomie" Bloomgarden, and she was selected as a finalist for the award! If you wish to look at her nomination biography or video, find it here.

We are hoping to return to campus and ultimate in the fall and are planning big things for our 15th season! Stay tuned!
